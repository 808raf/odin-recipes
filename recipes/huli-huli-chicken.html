Huli Huli Chicken

Description

Grilled chicken with a sweet and savory Huli Huli sauce, a Hawaiian barbecue classic.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken
    • thighs
  • 1/2 cup pineapple juice
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup ketchup
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp ginger, grated
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Pineapple slices for grilling

Steps

  1. In a bowl, whisk together pineapple juice, soy sauce, ketchup, honey, ginger, and garlic to make the Huli Huli sauce.
  2. Marinate chicken thighs in the sauce for at least 2 hours or overnight.
  3. Grill chicken until cooked through, basting with additional sauce.
  4. Grill pineapple slices.
  5. Serve grilled chicken with pineapple slices.
