Poke Bowl

A refreshing bowl featuring marinated raw fish, rice, and tropical flavors.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb sushi-grade ahi tuna, cubed
  • 2 cups cooked sushi rice
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tsp ginger, minced
  • 1 tsp garlic, minced
  • Green onions, sliced
  • Avocado, sliced
  • Mango, diced
  • Seaweed strips

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix soy sauce, sesame oil, rice vinegar, ginger, and garlic.
  2. Add tuna cubes and marinate for 15 minutes.
  3. Arrange rice in bowls, top with marinated tuna, avocado, mango, and seaweed strips.
  4. Garnish with sliced green onions.
